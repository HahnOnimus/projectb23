The Foundational Flaw

The web was originally built by Tim Berners-Lee for document sharing, not for commerce, discovery, or business optimization. That foundational design flaw persists today.

As a result, 400M+ SMEs are forced to operate across 5–8 fragmented platforms, wasting 30–50% of marketing spend, suffering brand inconsistency (83%), and flying blind with no real attribution or ROI tracking (68%).

Businesses, offers, and inventory remain buried—not because they lack value, but because the web was never designed to match businesses to demand.

The Solution: Business Visibility-as-a-Service

We are building Business Sky (B23) to solve this. B23 introduces Business Visibility-as-a-Service (BVaaS)—a neutral AI visibility layer that does for businesses what search engines did for web content.

Just as Google indexed documents and matched them to users, we index businesses, offers, products, and events, unifying all major platforms into a single always-on visibility engine.
