B23 Pioneer Program Guidelines

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

1. Overview of the Pioneer Program

These guidelines apply to participation in the B23 Pioneer Program. By becoming a Pioneer through a contribution, you agree to the terms described herein.

Important Clarification: The B23 Pioneer Program is designed to support the development of a global business visibility platform. It is NOT an investment opportunity, does NOT represent equity or ownership, and carries NO guarantee of financial returns. All references to potential future benefits, such as SAFE conversion, are at the sole discretion of the team and subject to change.

2. Nature of Pioneer Contributions

2.1 Contribution Purpose

A Pioneer contribution is an early commitment made in support of platform development. It does NOT constitute:

  • An investment in the company or platform
  • Equity, shares, or ownership rights
  • A loan or debt instrument
  • Any form of security

2.2 Use of Funds

All contributions received during the development phase are allocated exclusively toward building the platform. No refunds are available during this development period.

3. Potential Future SAFE Conversion

3.1 Optional Conversion Opportunity

At the sole discretion of the B23 team, Pioneers may be offered the chance to convert their contribution into a Simple Agreement for Future Equity (SAFE) during a future funding round, subject to separate agreements and applicable laws.

Potential Benefit: If offered, Pioneers may receive up to a 20% discount on SAFE conversion terms compared to later participants.

3.2 No Obligation or Guarantee

There is no obligation to offer SAFE conversion. Eligibility, terms, and availability remain entirely at the discretion of the B23 team.

4. Post-Launch Options

4.1 Refund Pathway

After platform launch, Pioneers who choose not to pursue any offered SAFE conversion may request a refund equivalent to their original contribution plus up to 25% additional return (capped accordingly), paid in installments over no less than six months.

4.2 Timing and Conditions

The refund option is available only within a limited window following launch and becomes unavailable once a Pioneer elects any conversion opportunity.

5. Recognition and Benefits

Pioneers receive recognition as early supporters, including permanent listing in the pioneer registry, media acknowledgments where applicable, advisory board participation, exclusive referrals, VIP event invitations, and other perks outlined during onboarding.

These benefits are provided as gestures of appreciation for early support and are subject to fulfillment based on platform progress.

6. Projections and Forward-Looking Statements

Any mentions of potential revenue sharing, returns, growth, or long-term outcomes are illustrative projections only. They are not promises or guarantees of performance.

Risk Acknowledgment: Participation involves uncertainty. Outcomes depend on successful development, market adoption, and execution. There is a possibility of receiving no financial benefit beyond the stated recognition and perks.

7. Program Management

The B23 team maintains transparent records of all Pioneer contributions and communications. Pioneer status is non-transferable without prior written approval.

8. Team Rights

The B23 team reserves the right to:

  • Modify program benefits or structure
  • Determine eligibility for any future opportunities
  • Adjust timelines and branding as needed
  • Terminate or alter the program at its discretion

9. General Guidelines

These guidelines may be updated from time to time. Continued participation implies acceptance of the latest version.

All matters are governed by the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria.

Final Note: The B23 Pioneer Program supports the creation of a transformative platform. Participation is an expression of belief in its vision. All benefits—tangible and recognition-based—are extended in good faith as the platform evolves.

By joining as a Pioneer, you confirm your understanding and acceptance of these guidelines.
